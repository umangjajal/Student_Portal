## FAQ

### Q: Can I extend the grace period?
**A:** Only if you request support. Admins can provide extensions for legitimate reasons (technical issues, etc.).

### Q: What if I add students AFTER grace period ends?
**A:** You can still add them, but you'll be marked as OVERDUE and may face restrictions. Always add within the 5-day window.

### Q: Can I change my plan during grace period?
**A:** Yes! Go to Subscription page and change plan anytime. Grace period reset if you upgrade/downgrade.

### Q: Do I have to add all students in one go?
**A:** No! You can add them gradually over the 5 days. Dashboard updates in real-time.

### Q: What if payment is late by 1 day?
**A:** Try to avoid this. Once marked OVERDUE, you may lose access. Always pay before deadline.

### Q: Can I see payment history?
**A:** Yes! Go to Subscription page â†’ "View Invoices" to see all payment history.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** UPI, Bank Transfer, Credit Card (via Invoice). UPI is fastest.

### Q: How long does payment confirmation take?
**A:** UPI: Instant. Bank Transfer: 24-48 hours. We send confirmation email when verified.
